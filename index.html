Meet

Stephen Tallamy

Stephen Tallamy is an experienced composer for TV, movies and other media.

His music has been used for TV shows, film and advertising with placements on major TV networks including NBC, USA, Big Ten Network, BBC One, Channel 4, Channel 5, Canal+ and ProSeiben.

He has recorded at the prestigious AIR Studios in London, home of countless film scores with players from London's talent scoring orchestras.

Based in Berkshire, Stephen writes music from a modern studio utilising a wide range of high quality virtual instruments, synthesizers and live musicians.

He has a deep understanding of media production with over 20 years working in the media industry and has been awarded a Emmy Award for technical innnovation.

He is one of the founders of Pianobook alongside Christian Henson.

He also loves Lego!
